Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be in genuine need of assistance
  • Proof of financial hardship may be required
  • Assistance availability depends on donations and funding
  • Priority given to families with children and seniors
  • Must be willing to provide necessary documentation

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get assistance?

Processing time varies based on the type of assistance requested, but we strive to respond to all requests within 5-7 business days. Emergency requests may be processed faster.

Do I need to provide proof of my situation?

For certain types of assistance (especially financial aid), we may require documentation such as proof of income, medical bills, or other relevant documents to verify your need.

Is there a limit to how often I can request help?

Yes, to ensure we can help as many people as possible, we typically limit assistance to once every 3 months for non-emergency requests, unless special circumstances apply.

What if my request is denied?

If your request is denied, we will provide information about other organizations that may be able to help. You may also reapply after 30 days if your situation changes.
