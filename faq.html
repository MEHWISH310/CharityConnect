Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I donate?

You can donate through our secure payment options listed on the donation page.

2. Can I donate anonymously?

Absolutely! You can choose to donate anonymously by selecting the checkbox on the donation form. Your personal details will remain confidential.

3. Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes, all donations are eligible for tax deductions. You will receive a receipt for tax purposes.

4. What payment methods are accepted?

We accept UPI, credit/debit cards, and net banking.

5. How is my donation used?

Your donation goes towards various causes, including food, education, and healthcare. We provide regular updates, impact reports, and financial statements. You can also visit our website to see where your contributions are making a difference.

6. Can I choose where my donation goes?

Yes, you can select a specific fund or choose 'General Donation' to support all causes.

7. Is my donation secure?

Yes, we use secure encryption for all transactions to ensure your data is safe.

8. How do I get a receipt?

Receipts are sent to your email after the donation is processed.

9. Can I set up a recurring donation?

Currently, we only support one-time donations, but we are working on adding this feature.

10. How can I contact support?

You can reach us through our contact page or email support@charityconnect.com.
